# 🏔️ Mountain Wave Calculator
**A React-based utility for mountain flying safety.**

[View the App Live](https://yuledev.github.io/FLT_Mountain_Wave_Calc/) | [Report an Issue](https://github.com/YuleDev/FLT_Mountain_Wave_Calc/issues)

---

## ✈️ Overview
Mountain waves (lee waves) can create vertical currents that exceed the climb performance of many general aviation aircraft. This tool helps pilots calculate:
* **Wavelength:** Distance between wave crests.
* **Vertical Velocity:** Estimated lift/sink rates.
* **Rotor Zones:** Identifying areas of severe turbulence.

## 📲 How to "Install" on Your Device
This is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. You don’t need an App Store to use it. Following these steps allows the app to work **offline** and appear on your home screen like a native app.

### 🍏 For iPad & iPhone (Recommended for Cockpit)
1. Open the [Calculator Link](https://yuledev.github.io/FLT_Mountain_Wave_Calc/) in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** icon (the square with an up arrow).
3. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add**. The icon will now appear next to ForeFlight or Garmin Pilot.

### 🤖 For Android
1. Open the link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots** in the top-right corner.
3. Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.

### 💻 For Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux)
1. Open the link in **Chrome** or **Edge**.
2. In the address bar, click the **Install icon** (looks like a small computer with a down arrow).
3. The app will now be available in your Applications folder/Start menu.

## ⚠️ Safety Warning
**FOR SUPPLEMENTAL PLANNING ONLY.** Atmospheric conditions in mountainous terrain are highly dynamic. Never rely solely on a calculator for terrain clearance. 
* If ridge-level winds exceed **25 knots**, expect significant wave action.
* Always have an "out" (a 45-degree turn toward lower terrain).
* This tool assumes standard lapse rates; actual conditions may vary.
